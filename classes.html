Aerobics

Our world-reknowned Aerobics programme has been featured in Vogue and remains a hot-topic in the fitness world! Our instructors have extensive dance backgrounds and choreograph exciting classes every week to boost your cardiovascular health and stamina! With a soundtrack exclusively featuring 80's music, you will forget you are "feeling the burn" in this high-energy class! All levels welcome!

Mondays-Sundays

Jazzercise

Jazzercise is a lower-impact cardio workout compared to Aerobics, focusing on cardio, rhythm and lengthening. Our instructors like to bring some classic Fosse moves for lengthening and strengthening alongside classic Jazzercise moves. This is a great conditioning workout to complement with any resistance exercise, complete with jazz hands. All levels welcome!


Mondays-Sundays

Spin

We have 40 of the most high-tech spin bikes to provide a challenge with steep inclines and realistic mountain challenges. Our experienced instructors will push you to your limit as you pedal to the metal. Our spin gym has a course projected on the surrounding walls so you feel like you are riding against the lycra in the Tour de France. You will gain strength and endurance in our hour-long classes.

Personal Training

We work closely with you to identify your fitness goals. Our trainers construct personalised fitness plans featuring weight-training, classes and nutritional advice. Our trainers ensure you follow a balanced plan to achieve that "hard body" and avoid burnout. Feel confident and strong with our 30 day Personal Trainer Galaxy Challenge.

Free Weights

Our free weight gym is equipped with state of the art equipment with spring-board floors and wall-to-wall mirrors. An on-call personal trainer is always on hand to offer tips and check alignment for optimal gains and training safety.

Sauna and Showers

Our Alpine cedar saunas are the ultimate luxury for post-workout relief or as a stand-alone indulgence for those off days. Infused with eucalyptus oil, each sauna offers privacy and luxury. We have a communal sauna and four private saunas which can be pre-booked.
Our showers use filtered high-water pressure to massage hard-worked muscles and for our guests to feel ultimately clean. We provide complimentary organic shampoos, conditioners and bodywash which we created in partnership with Oula Organix. Our towel service provides incredibly soft linen/cotton towels for your pleasure.
